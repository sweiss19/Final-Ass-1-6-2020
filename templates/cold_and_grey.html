Cold and Grey

{% endblock %} {% block poem %}

I am cold and grey
I wonder if I’ll ever change
I hear the bundle of dust moving around
I see a rusty chest cracking
I want to stay the way I am now
I am cold and grey.

I pretend to be an old, abandoned chest
I feel some grey fuzzy mice tickling me
I touch the silence of the darkness
I worry that a spark will light up the room
I cry about the day that I will be discovered
I am cold and grey.

I understand that I’m not the usual
I say, let the spookiness spread its wings even more
I dream about a monster coming to greet me
I try to make the best out of my life
I hope to stay in my place as long as I live
I am cold and grey.
