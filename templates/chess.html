A Game of Chess

Two brains at work
Sharing a common goal
On opposite ends
They play important roles.

Competing through mind
Intelligence in motion
Creating strategies
Following their intuition.

Their aim is known
The king at target
To beat their opponent
They will never forget.

Many pieces involved
So equally important
Not one could be let go
A mindset so adamant.

A pawn may fall
But the king stands erect
It holds the victory
It's kingdom, it protects.

The game is over
If the leader is hit
A loss so hurtful
It is difficult to admit.
The game is always present
Its battle occurs daily
Have the correct mindset
And your heart will win constantly!
