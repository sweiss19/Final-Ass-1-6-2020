A Journey

{% endblock %} {% block poem %}

A journey
Endless stones laying ahead
Coming our way
Every day in this world.
Our knees
Are scraped and bloody
From constant falls
They hurt and feel tired.
We proceed
On our given paths
With continuous struggle
One leg at a time.
Suspicion rising
Trying to spot enemies
With bad intentions
That wish to destroy us.
But no –
Their plan won’t work
We are brave
For we will overcome them.
We hold
The flag of truth
With everlasting pride
To prove our enemies wrong.
This day
We all stand together
We are heroes
For winning the untold battles.
It’s hard
To taste the honey
And feel sweetness
When surrounded by buzzing bees.
But yes –
We received the honey
It is pure
It is a bee’s creation.
We sing
The song of heroism
We are champions
For not losing our morality.
Like soldiers
Fighting for their country
Some are wounded
But their nation is protected.
Our dignity
Is what we cherish
Until we emerge
Clean from the ongoing mess!
